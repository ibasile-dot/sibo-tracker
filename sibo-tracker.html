Grocery Lists

Low Fermentation Diet — Dairy Free

🚫 Avoid / Minimise

✕ Onions, garlic, celery, squash
✕ Apple, pear, peach, watermelon
✕ Whole wheat, rye, brown rice
✕ Beans & legumes
✕ ALL dairy — milk, cheese, yoghurt, butter, ice cream
✕ Agave, high fructose corn syrup, sucralose, sugar alcohols (-ol endings)
✕ Nuts & seeds
✕ Raw green salad & raw vegetables
✕ Alcohol (during antibiotic phase)

✓ Safe to Eat

✓ White bread, white pasta, white rice, white potato
✓ ALL vegetables — cooked until soft, never raw/al dente
✓ Eggs (at home only)
✓ Chicken, turkey, fish, seafood, tofu, tempeh
✓ Oat milk, coconut milk, almond milk
✓ Olive oil, coconut oil
✓ Bananas, blueberries, strawberries, oranges, grapes
✓ Matcha, coffee, black tea, herbal tea, water
✓ Soy sauce / tamari, ginger, basil, cinnamon, lemon
✓ Maple syrup, jam (no added sugar, safe fruit only)
✓ Rice cakes

💡 Eating Tips

Cook all vegetables until soft — never al dente or raw
Space meals 4–5 hours apart
No calories 2 hours before bed
12-hour overnight fast (matcha, water, black coffee/tea ok)
Batch cook: 3 portions per batch = 1 dinner + 2 lunches
